Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using InnoConnect.

How do I sign up as an innovator?

You can sign up by clicking the "Get Started" button on our homepage and selecting the "Innovator" role. Follow the prompts to create your account and start submitting your projects.

How does InnoConnect match me with investors?

We use advanced algorithms to analyze your project details and match you with investors whose interests align with your innovation. You’ll also get recommendations to improve your project’s visibility.

Is there a fee to use InnoConnect?

InnoConnect offers a free plan with basic features. We also have premium plans for advanced features like priority investor matching and enhanced project management tools. Check our pricing page for details.

How secure is my data on InnoConnect?

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we comply with international privacy standards to protect your information.

Can I collaborate with other innovators on the platform?

Yes! Our Collaborative Space allows you to connect with other innovators, share ideas, and work together on projects.
